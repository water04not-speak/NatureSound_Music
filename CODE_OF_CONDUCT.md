# Code of Conduct

NatureSound Music aims to be a friendly, practical open source learning space for HarmonyOS, ArkTS, ArkUI, and music app engineering.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient with contributors at different experience levels.
- Give feedback that is specific, constructive, and focused on the work.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its learning goals.
- Respect copyright, privacy, and platform policy boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Submitting copyrighted media, credentials, or private project files.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.
